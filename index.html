Instructions

The piano supports a range from C2 to E7. Use your keys on your keyboard to play

    • Corrosponding Natural Keys
    • Num 1 - C
    • Num 2 - D
    • Num 3 - E
    • Num 4 - F
    • Num 5 - G
    • Num 6 - A
    • Num 7 - B
    • Num 8 - High C
    • Num 9 - High D
    • Num 0 - High E
    • Corrosponding Accidental Keys
    • Q - C#/Db
    • W - D#/Eb
    • E - F#/Gb
    • R - G#/Ab
    • T - A#/Bb
    • Y - High C#/Db
    • U - High D#/Eb
    • Switching Octaves
    • J - Move up one octave
    • K - Move down one octave
